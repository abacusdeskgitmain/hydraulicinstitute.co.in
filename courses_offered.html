Purpose 

The purpose of the training is to make participants capable of maintaining and troubleshoot the hydraulic equipment and systems by understanding how and why all the various components work. That is all these courses are all about. This naturally involves knowing tools and their proper application,, basic principles and functions of assemblies and sub-assemblies of a system or equipment. The short term courses are perfect for enhancing skills of existing maintenance personnel whereas 3 months and 6 months courses are for fresh Diploma holders and ITI’s who want to make a carrier in highly demanding industries where the availability of skills is scarce.

Unaffiliated and Non-Commercial

Unlike some training experiences one won’t get a sales presentation – all one gets is an honest, commercially unbiased education. We do not engage in the practice of selling or promoting any company’s products or services so one knows that by attending our courses, ones time and money is well spent.

Who Should Attend

These courses are designed for anyone who needs to understand hydraulic components and systems for work in industrial and mobile equipment applications. Attendees can come from a wide variety of industries, skill-levels, company sizes and backgrounds, so if one is not sure that will fit in or will benefit from these classes, don’t worry- he will- as long as his work  involves hydraulics! People who will benefit from attending these courses include maintenance personnel including mechanics, apprentices(fresher ITI’s and Diploma holders), operators, fitters, supervisors, all multicraft personnel, plant managers/ engineers.

The courses/ program are designed for 3 days, 5 days and 3 months depending on qualifications and experiences of personnel. he 3 days and 5 days program are suitable to candidates from the field with hands-on experience whereas 3 months course/ programmes are recommended for fresh Diploma holders and ITI’s respectively. These long term courses are designed as such that these candidates can be a part of highly skilled & scarcely available industrial/ mobile hydraulic service industries.

 

3 Days Basic Hydraulics Course

  • What does Hydraulics mean?
  • Pascal’s Law
  • Pumps
  • Motors
  • Valves-Safety (relief),Pressure regulating, Directional & Proportional valve, Fittings, and Hoses
  • Cylinders
  • Hydraulic reservoirs, Coolers, Oils, Filters and Maintenance systems
  • Control valve banks and circuits. with terminology, Symbols and Nomograph
  • Basic Troubleshooting

Rs. 6000/-  per participant for 3 days (with free tea and lunch )

Methodology
The theoretical & practical concepts on technology would be elaborated through audio visuals, case studies and workshop training.   

5 Days Mobile / Industrial Hydraulics Course

  • Hydrostatic transmissions

  • Joysticks

Benefits of above courses

  • Participants will develop clear understanding of hydraulics from basics
  • The course would give theoretical and practical knowledge of principles of operation  and functions of hydraulic elements and systems
  • Enable participants to handle hydraulic system’s maintenance and the ability to read prints and drawings
